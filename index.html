TransmissionMap

This unofficial, community-built map of the North American energy infrastructure comprises data aggregated from public sources and may be incomplete, outdated, or inaccurate. It is provided for educational and informational purposes only.

Do not use for engineering, operational, safety, or regulatory decisions. No warranty of any kind is provided.

THIS MAP AND ALL ASSOCIATED DATA ARE PROVIDED "AS IS" AND "AS AVAILABLE." NO WARRANTY OF ANY KIND, EXPRESS OR IMPLIED, IS PROVIDED. IN NO EVENT SHALL THE PROVIDER BE LIABLE FOR ANY CLAIM, DAMAGES, OR OTHER LIABILITY ARISING FROM USE OF OR RELIANCE ON THIS MAP OR ITS DATA.

See the README on GitHub for data sources, limitations, and licensing.

⚠ Live wildfire data is stale

The live wildfire data is — old — beyond the safe limit. The hourly feed may be broken or delayed, so the active fire, smoke, and incident layers have been turned off to avoid showing dangerously outdated fire positions.

Do not rely on stale fire data for any safety or evacuation decision. It will re-enable automatically once a fresh update arrives. Meanwhile, WECC's Wildfire Risk Dashboard shows current wildfires near western transmission infrastructure.
